The Chocolate Future ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or engage with our investment platform.

- Investment inquiry details
- Company or organizational affiliation
- Communication preferences

### Automatically Collected Information
Our servers may automatically collect:
- IP addresses and device identifiers

### Third-Party Services
We do not use third-party analytics or tracking services that collect personally identifiable information. Any data collected is handled in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data only when necessary to:
